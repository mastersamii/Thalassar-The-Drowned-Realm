# 🌊 The Realm of Thalassar

> *"The Sea Remembers Every Name."*

Welcome to the official digital campaign companion for **The Realm of Thalassar**, a custom Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition campaign setting.

---

# Overview

Thalassar is a world without land.

Civilization survives upon:

- Floating Cities
- Sky Kingdoms
- Merchant Fleets
- Pirate Armadas
- Remote Ocean Settlements
- The Underwater Kingdom of Atlantis

Players awaken beneath the waves inside bodies that are not their own and slowly uncover a conspiracy stretching across the entire world.
