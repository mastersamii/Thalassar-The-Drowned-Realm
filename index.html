Atlantis

Atlantis

Atlantis is the greatest city beneath the sea. Its coral towers glow with bioluminescent light, its streets are watched by the Tidewardens, and its citizens believe they live in the safest civilization in the world.

Trident Palace: Seat of King Aurelion Veyrath IX.
Tidewarden Headquarters: Command center of Atlantis's military force.
Silvered Market: The living heart of the city.
Scholar’s Deep: Archives, research, restricted records, and missing histories.
The Drowned Ward: Abandoned, restricted, and full of secrets.
